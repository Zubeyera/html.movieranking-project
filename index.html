The Best Movies According to Genevieve

My Top Movies of all Time

Flawsome

A well told series of young independent friends in search of true happiness.

Anikulapo

Natural, culture influenced movie which has a sequel. The actors delivered their roles uniquely and the movie is packed with emotions.

Love in a Pandemic

A unique love Story of a young Customer Representative in a middle of a Pandemic.

Gangs of Lagos

My one time favoite. An elaborate slow-mo fight scenes, and the constancy of close-ups when characters express grief. Fully packed with lessons.

A Tribe called Judah

A story of five brothers who come together to save their mother by doing what they thought was best. Well delivered emotions through the Actors.
